## Customer Sentiment Analysis — Jewelry

**Findings & Insights**  
- **Product Durability (32.5%)**: Primary driver of dissatisfaction; issues include clasp breakage, tarnishing, and stone loss  
- **Customer Service (27.5%)**: Warranty denials, refund friction, and lack of responsiveness contribute to churn  
- **Declining Quality (17.5%)**: Long-term customers report noticeable degradation in newer products  

- Durability issues are strongly associated with high-severity complaints and repeat negative experiences  
- Ineffective customer service prevents resolution and accelerates churn  
- Perceived quality decline threatens long-term brand loyalty  

  **Recommendations**  
  - Improve product durability through enhanced materials and quality control  
  - Invest in customer support (staffing or AI-driven solutions) to reduce friction  
  - Align current product quality with legacy standards to retain loyal customers  

---

**Approach, Tools & Objective**  
- **Objective**: Identify key drivers of customer dissatisfaction and churn through analysis of unstructured consumer reviews, and translate findings into actionable recommendations  

- Aggregated and structured multi-platform review data  
- Applied Generative AI (Claude, Perplexity) for text analysis and classification  
- Categorized feedback by pain point, severity, customer type, and churn risk  
- Developed a dashboard to visualize trends and patterns  

  **Deliverables & Impact**  
  - Interactive dashboard visualizing pain points, severity, and churn risk  
  - Structured dataset derived from unstructured review data  

  Supports data-driven decision-making to reduce churn, improve customer experience, and strengthen brand perception.
