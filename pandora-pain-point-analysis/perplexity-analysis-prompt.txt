Analyze customer reviews from at least 100 recent reviews of popular Pandora jewelry products (such as charms, bracelets, rings, necklaces, and earrings).
Identify the top 8–10 pain points customers mention most frequently. For each pain point, include:
The specific issue (e.g., product quality, sizing, delivery, customer service, etc.)
How frequently it appears in reviews (percentage or frequency)
1–2 example quotes from actual customers illustrating the issue
Organize the findings as a clear, structured summary that highlights common themes and potential areas for product or service improvement.
