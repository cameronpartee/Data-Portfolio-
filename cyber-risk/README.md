**Findings & Insights**  
- **Financial Impact Patterns**: While ransomware dominates headlines, analysis of 850+ cyber incidents shows other threat types contribute the majority of financial losses  
- **Industry Underinsurance**: Many sectors are significantly underinsured relative to potential losses, increasing exposure to large-scale events  
- **Emerging Threat Trends**: AI-related attack vectors and automation vulnerabilities are becoming more prominent, suggesting an evolving risk landscape  

- Data indicates that headline risks are not always the highest financial risk  
- Underinsured industries are at higher risk of catastrophic losses  
- Early identification and mitigation of emerging threats can reduce potential financial exposure  

  **Recommendations**  
  - Conduct comprehensive risk assessments to identify underinsured areas  
  - Prioritize mitigation strategies for high-impact, high-likelihood threats beyond just ransomware  
  - Invest in AI-driven monitoring and predictive analytics to anticipate emerging threats  

---

**Approach, Tools & Objective**  
- **Objective**: Forecast potential financial burden from cyber incidents and inform strategic risk management decisions  

- Analyzed a dataset of 850+ corporate cyber incidents  
- Used Python for data cleaning, aggregation, and statistical analysis  
- Visualized loss distribution and risk exposure by industry using dashboards  
- Identified patterns to forecast financial impact under varying scenarios  

  **Deliverables & Impact**  
  - Interactive dashboard showing industry-level risk, loss distribution, and emerging threat trends  
  - Structured dataset supporting financial risk modeling  

  Supports proactive decision-making to reduce exposure, optimize insurance coverage, and prioritize investments in cybersecurity infrastructure.
